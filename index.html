My professional trajectory encompasses diverse roles, ranging from serving as a waitress to assuming responsibilities as a student mentor, Spanish translator for Riverside County, and ultimately, a college ambassador for a non-profit organization. Each phase has contributed significantly to my skill set and professional growth.

Commencing as a waitress, I acquired essential skills such as multitasking, effective communication, and thriving in a fast-paced environment. This foundational experience instilled in me a strong work ethic and adaptability, laying the groundwork for subsequent professional endeavors. Transitioning to the role of a student mentor, I undertook the responsibility of assisting and guiding students, particularly those whose primary language is Spanish, through their educational studies. This role demanded active engagement during homework sessions and the provision of valuable assistance in revising assignments. It underscored the importance of mentorship and honed my interpersonal and leadership skills.

My trajectory then led me to a position as a part-time Spanish translator for the Riverside County Black Chamber of Commerce. In less than six months, my role expanded, providing me with the opportunity to collaborate with the community outreach program. This extension involved engaging with Latino business owners, facilitating communication, and creating targeted promotional materials to support their small businesses. This experience not only showcased my linguistic proficiency but also highlighted my strategic thinking and ability to contribute to community initiatives. Simultaneously, I was approached by a scholarship program of which I am an alum, offering me the role of a college ambassador for the University of California, Riverside (UCR). As a college ambassador, my responsibilities include leading, guiding, and supporting a group of fellow alumni in navigating the complexities of higher education. This role requires organizational prowess, as evidenced by my coordination of mixers, social events, and educational programs to foster a sense of community and support among scholarship recipients.
